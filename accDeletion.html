Account Deletion

What happens when you delete your account?

Deleting your account means:

  • All your personal information will be permanently removed from our system.
  • You will lose access to your account and any associated data.
  • You won't be able to recover your account once it's deleted.

Please make sure you've backed up any important data before proceeding.
